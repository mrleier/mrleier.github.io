Hotel Room Block

The Williamsburg Lodge which is the closest hotel to both the ceremony and reception locations.

WILLIAMSBURG LODGE
310 South England Street Williamsburg VA 23185
(757) 220-7976

To make a reservation, please call (757) 220-7976 and ask for available options in our room block. The booking code to use is 44974. There are two categories of rooms available Lodge Cozy and Lodge Deluxe. Cozy rooms are $119 per night and have a single Queen bed. Deluxe rooms are $165 per night and have two Queen beds.

Alternate Accommodations

There are numerous hotels located a short drive from the ceremony and reception locations. The Fairfield Inn & Suites on Richmond Road and the La Quinta on Bypass Road are both great alternatives to the Williamsburg Lodge.
